Suzanne Knop

Bio

Suzanne Knop is a Digital Media Design major currently attending the University of Pennsylvania in Philadelphia, but originally from Poughkeepsie, New York.

ABOUT
This website started out as a project of mine to learn web design (it's made completely from scratch!), though now it has become more of a portfolio for my artwork and other projects. This site is constantly transforming as I learn more web development techniques and update my work, so I encourage you to check back every once in a while!

I love art, I love computer science, I love listening to music and knitting and playing soccer and reading poetry and drinking coffee and reading. Right now, I am still in the process of exploring my interests in the field of computer science, computer graphics, and art!

Fun Fact: The name of my website comes from Picasso's La Bouteille de Suze (Bottle of Suze), of which I like very much and also resonate with because of my name.
